Services

Supporting your product development

I am a geospatial data scientist with over a decade of experience in the remote sensing of plants. I develop methods, processes and softare for turning sensor data into useful information.

I provide a range of services including system design, feasibility studies, remote sensing support for research projects and technical consultancy.

Find out more about my projects:

  • Invasive plant management at scale
  • Tree diseases in UK farmland

Data emulation for new satellite earth-observation systems

I provide end-to-end emulators customised for future missions or systems. Depending on the task, these can be configured to provide accurate target, atmospheric and sensor simulations. Data products can be used to develop predictive models prior to launch, giving end users confidence that the operational data will meet their requirements.

Find out more about my projects:

  • Simulating satellite data for a tree-focused mission
