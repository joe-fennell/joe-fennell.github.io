Services

Supporting your product development

I am a geospatial data consultant with over a decade of experience in remote sensing research. My specialist areas are in remote sensing of plants, machine learning and image analysis systems. I can support projects from imager development through to end-user interface, and every stage in between.

Designing new satellite earth-observation systems

I provide end-to-end emulators customised to your mission or system. Depending on the task, these can be configured to provide accurate target, atmospheric and sensor simulations. Data products can be used to develop predictive models prior to launch, giving your end users confidence that your data will meet their requirements.

Sourcing the right data for your project
